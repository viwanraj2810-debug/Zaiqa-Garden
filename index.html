Awards & Honours

Tucked inside a lush open garden in Sector 18, Zaiqa Garden has been Noida's favourite spot for family get-togethers, romantic evenings, and celebratory feasts since 2017. Our open-air seating under fairy lights makes every meal an occasion.

Every recipe is a tribute to the rich culinary heritage of Awadh and Mughlai kitchens — slow-cooked, generously spiced, and served with genuine warmth.
